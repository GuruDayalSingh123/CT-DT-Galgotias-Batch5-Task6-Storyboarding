## Task 06 – Storyboarding

# Product: AI Study Assistant App

## Storyboard Summary

### Scene 1 – Problem

A student struggles to organize study materials, manage deadlines, and prepare effectively for exams due to poor planning and scattered resources.

**Emotion:** 😟 Frustrated

---

### Scene 2 – Discovery

The student discovers the AI Study Assistant App through social media, online recommendations, and friends.

**Emotion:** 🤔 Curious

---

### Scene 3 – Registration

The student downloads the app, creates an account, and completes the profile setup.

**Emotion:** 😊 Hopeful

---

### Scene 4 – Goal Setting

The student sets academic goals, uploads notes, selects subjects, and creates a personalized study plan.

**Emotion:** 💪 Motivated

---

### Scene 5 – AI Assistance

The AI generates summaries, answers questions, creates study schedules, recommends learning resources, and provides personalized guidance.

**Emotion:** 📚 Productive

---

### Scene 6 – Progress Tracking

The student monitors learning progress through performance analytics, daily streaks, and improvement reports.

**Emotion:** 😌 Confident

---

### Scene 7 – Success

The student performs better in exams, achieves higher grades, and recommends the AI Study Assistant App to classmates and friends.

**Emotion:** 🎉 Satisfied

---

## Conclusion

The storyboard illustrates how the AI Study Assistant App transforms a student's learning journey—from confusion and stress to organized study habits, improved academic performance, and increased confidence through intelligent and personalized assistance.
